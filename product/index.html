    Technical Specifications

    Technology: Near-Infrared Transmissivity - Painless

    Required Smartphone Application: GLUCUBE APP for Android and iOS

    Battery: Rechargeable Li-Po

    Charging Connector: USB Type-C

    Charging cable: supplied with the product.
    USB Type A - USB Type C

    Charger: Not included with the device.
    - Output voltage: 5 V = (+-5%)
    - Output current: 200 mA (minimum)
    - Output connector: USB Type A
    - Isolated with 2 MOOP from the power supply network

    Wireless Connection: Low Energy Bluetooth (BLE) - 4.2

    Maximum Operating Range: 2 m between the device and smartphone


    To install the application on your smartphone, the following requirements must be met:

    Android Requirements

    Android Version: Android 9 or later

    CPU Speed: Quad Core 1.2GHz or higher

    RAM: 2 GB (minimum)

    Internal Storage: 16 GB (minimum)

    Connection: Wi-Fi or 4G (data service)

    Screen Size: 5’’ (minimum)

    Minimum Screen Resolution: HD (1,280 x 720 pixels)

    iOS Requirements

    iOS Version: iOS 15.1 or later

    Minimum iPhone Version: iPhone 7

    • DO NOT use GLUCUBE values as a reference for medication or to modify treatment plans. If glucose values are below or above established ranges, use another self-diagnosis method or consult a professional before making any decisions.
    • Requires the installation of a mobile application on a smartphone.
    • Requires internet access to download the application and use the product.

    Contraindications

    This system is not suitable for:

    • People with uncontrolled type 1 diabetes, type 2 diabetes, metabolic diseases, and diseases requiring chronic treatment.
    • Individuals using artificial nails or nail polish.
    • Individuals with finger injuries, such as deformities or calluses.
    • Individuals experiencing tremors.
    • Individuals with visual or circulatory disabilities (e.g., Raynaud's).
    • Patients in shock or critical condition.
    • Pregnant women.
    • Individuals under 18 years of age.

    Usage Limitations

    This system is not recommended for:

    • Patients with disabilities without assistance.
    • Prognosis or diagnosis of diabetes.
