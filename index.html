FAQ

FAQ

GLUCUBE consists of an infrared device, a mobile application and artificial intelligence.

  • The measuring device uses infrared light to obtain the blood glucose concentration through the finger.
  • The application allows the visualisation and monitoring of the data recorded by the device.
  • The artificial intelligence that is housed in our servers, clusters, databases, internal networks, the algorithm that estimates the blood glucose value and our mesh of microservices for the numerous functionalities offered by the application.

The GLUCUBE technology that makes it possible to obtain blood glucose levels easily, comfortably and painlessly is near infrared spectroscopy (NIRS). This beam of infrared light, emitted when the finger is inserted into the device, detects glucose molecules, and the GLUCUBE algorithm displays the measurement of blood sugar levels on the app, without the need for capillary punctures or other devices inserted into the skin.
